Culture & Cuisine

Asmara, Eritrea's capital, boasts a culture rich in historical influences, particularly Italian, and a diverse cuisine reflecting this blend, as well as traditional Eritrean flavors. Cuisine is central to Eritrean culture, featuring shared meals where stews are scooped up with injera, a sourdough flatbread. Italian influences are evident in dishes like pasta al sugo e berbere and coffee specialties like espresso and cappuccino.

Travel Tips

  1. Best time to visit: October to March (dry season)
  2. Nearest airport: Asmara International Airport (ASM)
  3. Currency: Eritrean Nakfa (ERN)
  4. Visa: Required for most visitors; check embassy details
